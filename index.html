Built on Play.

At Playsphere, play is not an afterthought. It is the foundation of everything we do. We design outdoor spaces that are purposeful, inclusive and beautiful. Spaces that excite curious kids, bring families together and become landmarks in their own right.

Step 01
Concept
We start with a conversation. Understanding the space, the community and the vision before a single line is drawn.
Step 02
Design
Every space is designed from scratch, tailored to the site and the people. Never off the shelf, always uniquely Playsphere.
Step 03
Play
A holistic approach to designing play. Balancing active, imaginative, sensorial, and inclusive experiences that inspire exploration.
Step 04
Build
From certified raw materials to precision manufacturing, every element is built to last and built with care.
